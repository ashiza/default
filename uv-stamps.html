UV & Security Stamps

UV invisible ink stamps are specially formulated with transparent inks that are invisible to the naked eye under normal lighting conditions. When exposed to ultraviolet (UV) light, the stamp reveals a bright, clear mark that is highly visible and difficult to replicate. This makes UV stamps an essential tool for security marking, document authentication, and event entry verification.

UV Invisible Ink Stamps

  • Invisible Under Normal Light: Perfect for discreet security marking
  • Visible Under UV Light: Reveals a bright, clear mark for verification
  • Anti-Counterfeiting: Adds an extra layer of security to documents
  • Event Entry: Quickly verify attendees with a UV torch
  • Document Protection: Mark confidential papers and legal documents
  • Water Resistant: Durable marking that withstands handling

Quick Drying Inks

Our quick-drying stamp inks are engineered for high-volume, fast-paced environments where smudging cannot be tolerated. Ideal for self-inking stamps, pre-inked stamps, and hand stamps.

  • Instant Drying: Dries within 3-10 seconds on most surfaces
  • Water Resistant: Once dry, ink provides excellent water resistance
  • Versatile Use: Suitable for glossy paper, packaging, and documents
  • Smudge-Free: Prevents smudging in high-volume stamping
