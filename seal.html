Trodat Notary Seal

The Trodat Notary Seal 40mm is a self-inking seal designed for notaries who require clear, precise impressions on legal documents, certificates, and official paperwork. With a 40mm diameter, it delivers a large, consistent imprint, ensuring legal compliance. Built for durability and high-volume use, it provides crisp impressions every time. For those who prefer an embossed mark, the Trodat Notary Embossing Press creates a raised impression, adding authenticity to documents. Whether using the self-inking seal or the embosser, the Trodat Notary Seal 40mm ensures quality, reliability, and professionalism in all notarial work.

Why the Trodat Notary Seal

The Trodat Notary Embossing Seal is the perfect choice for notaries who need to create a high-quality, raised impression on official documents. Known for its precision and durability, this embossing press delivers an authentic, professional touch to legal papers, certificates, and contracts. The raised, tactile impression ensures that your documents stand out, providing an extra layer of security and credibility. Designed for ease of use and long-lasting performance, the Trodat Notary Embossing Seal is a trusted tool that guarantees consistent, reliable results every time, making it an essential item for any notary looking to maintain the highest standards of professionalism.
