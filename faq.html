What is a UV security stamp and how does it work?

A UV security stamp uses specially formulated invisible ink that is only visible under ultraviolet (UV) light. Under normal lighting conditions, the stamp mark is completely invisible. When exposed to a UV torch or black light, the mark becomes clearly visible.

Common uses include:

  • Event entry and re-entry verification
  • Document security and anti-counterfeiting
  • Confidential document marking
  • Inventory and asset marking
  • Ticket authentication
What is the difference between self-inking and traditional hand stamps?

Self-Inking Stamps: These stamps have a built-in ink pad that automatically re-inks the stamp after each impression. They are ideal for high-volume, repetitive stamping and offer clean, consistent results without the need for a separate ink pad.

Traditional Hand Stamps: These stamps require a separate ink pad. They are more cost-effective for low-volume use and offer the flexibility to use different ink colors by switching pads. They are also available in custom sizes and with wooden or plastic handles.

How do I maintain and refill my self-inking stamp?

To maintain your self-inking stamp:

  • Store the stamp in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Clean the stamp plate occasionally with a soft cloth and stamp cleaner
  • When impressions become faint, it's time to refill the ink

Refilling the ink:

  • We stock genuine replacement ink cartridges for all Trodat and Colop models
  • Contact us to order the correct refill cartridge for your specific model
  • Installation is simple and quick—we can guide you through the process
What is the difference between Trodat, Colop, and Parnami stamps?

Trodat: A premium Austrian brand known for high-quality self-inking stamps, date stamps, and notary seals. Trodat stamps are durable, eco-friendly (made from recycled materials), and feature easy ink cartridge replacement. Popular models include 4727, 4913, 5460, and 2910.

Colop: Another leading Austrian brand offering high-quality self-inking stamps. The C60 model is popular for large office stamps with logo capabilities.

Parnami / Prime: A cost-effective alternative that offers durable, none self-inking date stamps. These are ideal for businesses looking for quality stamping at a lower price point.
